Duotone Variations

Goal: Refine the "Duotone with Accent" style. Here are 6 variations exploring different ways to use the accent color, stroke, and fill.

1

Original Duotone

The selected baseline. Dark grey strokes with soft blue fills.

  • Stroke: Dark Grey (currentColor)
  • Fill: Blue (#646cff) at 15% opacity
  • Accent: Occasional blue stroke
  • Balanced and professional
